The model

Three parties.
One smart network.

Urjio orchestrates everything — from lease agreements to installation to payments — so each stakeholder earns without the operational burden.

Step 01
🏢

Landowners lease space

Apartment complexes, commercial plots, and parking facilities lease their unused spots to Urjio. Zero capital outlay, zero maintenance responsibility. We handle permits, installation, and day-to-day operations entirely.

Step 02
⚡

Charger partners deploy pods

We partner with EVRE, Bolt.Earth, Fortum GLIDA, and others to lease their charging hardware. Partners earn guaranteed revenue per pod without the site-finding burden — Urjio delivers pre-approved, permit-ready locations.

Step 03
🚗

Drivers charge and pay

EV owners find, book, and pay via the Urjio app. Revenue is automatically split in real time — the landowner gets their share, the charger partner gets theirs, and Urjio retains a transparent platform fee.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 April 2026

What we collect

When you use Urjio's website or app we collect your name, email address, phone number, and general location in Hyderabad. For EV drivers we also collect charging session data (duration, kWh, payment amount) to calculate revenue splits and process payouts.

How we use it

We use your information to facilitate lease agreements with landowners, coordinate charger deployments with partner companies, process payments and payouts, and improve the Urjio platform. We do not sell your data to any third party under any circumstances.

Data storage

All data is stored on servers within India, compliant with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDPA). Payment data is handled by RBI-compliant payment processors and is never stored on Urjio's own servers.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing [email protected]. We will respond within 7 business days.

Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies only — for session management and anonymous analytics. We do not use advertising cookies or share data with ad networks.

Terms of Service

Last updated: 1 April 2026

1. The platform

Urjio Energy Pvt Ltd ("Urjio") operates a three-sided marketplace connecting landowners, EV charger companies, and EV drivers in Hyderabad, India. By using our website, app, or services you agree to these terms.

2. Landowner agreements

Landowners who lease space to Urjio will sign a separate Lease Agreement detailing the revenue share percentage, payment schedule, guaranteed monthly minimum (if applicable), and termination conditions. These Terms supplement but do not replace that agreement.

3. Charger partner agreements

EV charger companies leasing pods through Urjio will sign a Hardware Partnership Agreement specifying pod counts, utilisation targets, revenue share, maintenance responsibilities, and exclusivity terms (if any). Urjio actively discourages blanket exclusivity clauses.

4. EV driver terms

Drivers using Urjio charging stations pay per kWh as displayed in the app at time of booking. Prices may vary by location, time of day, and charger type. Urjio reserves the right to adjust tariffs with 7 days' notice via app notification.

5. Revenue sharing

Revenue split ranges (landowner 25–30%, charger partner 20–25%, Urjio 45–50%) are indicative. Exact percentages are fixed in individual agreements and visible in your Urjio dashboard. Payouts are processed monthly via NEFT/IMPS within 5 business days of month close.

6. Uptime and liability

Urjio maintains a 99% uptime SLA and will credit affected sessions in the event of unplanned downtime exceeding this threshold. Urjio is not liable for damage to vehicles arising from charger malfunction beyond what is covered by our hardware partners' warranty terms.

7. Governing law

These Terms are governed by the laws of India. Any disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of courts in Hyderabad, Telangana.
